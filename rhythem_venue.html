Rhythm Venue

A collection of retail venues

This project is proposed as an architectural reflection towards the Retail-tainment trend. An iconic collection of showcase venues, in various sizes and shapes, is targeting middle-class shoppers (the primary consumers of fast fashion in Oxford Street).

These venues employ various interactive and engaging elements, such as brand campaigns, product demonstrations, interactive games, and digital experiences such as virtual and augmented reality, to create an immersive and memorable shopping experience.
